Privacy Policy

Welcome to PsyNoteX. Your privacy is our highest priority.

1. Data Collection

PsyNoteX does not collect any personal data. All your notes, mood entries, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences).

2. No Internet Access

PsyNoteX works completely offline. We do not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

3. No Permissions Required

PsyNoteX requires zero device permissions. We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device feature.

4. Data Storage

Your journal entries, mood data, tags, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is never backed up to any cloud service by the app itself.

5. PIN Security

If you choose to enable a PIN lock, the PIN is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted anywhere.

6. Analytics

All analytics (mood trends, insights, heatmaps) are computed entirely on your device using your local data. No analytics data is sent externally.

7. Children's Privacy

PsyNoteX does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this is inherently satisfied.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes.
